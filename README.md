# Power Outage — Quick Play (Assistant Managers)

**Version:** 1.1
**Owner (ODD):** AMOD (Assistant Manager on Duty)
**DoD:** Everyone safe • Food safe • Cash logged • Utility ticket made • AAR filed within 24h

---

## Read This When Lights Die

**If in doubt:** **CLOSE · LOCK · LOG · CALL**

### T+0–2 MIN — STABILIZE (SAFETY FIRST)

1. **Call it:** “Power outage — Play running.”
2. **Clear guests** to bright/safe area; escort from restrooms.
3. **Stop hot equipment.** Kill fryers/grills.
4. **Post signs** at door & drive‑thru: *Temporary Power Outage*.
5. **Lock DT window.** Pause service.
   **Proof:** 1 photo of signs + start time in log.

### T+2–10 MIN — SECURE FOOD & CASH

6. **Seal cold units. Do NOT open.**
7. **Start Manual Sales Log** (paper) Payment in cash . 
8. **Call utility** → get **ticket # & ETA**.
9. **Notify ops chat:** store, time, ticket #, ETA, open/closed status.
   **Proof:** Ticket # + screenshot to ops.

### T+10–60 MIN — DECIDE: LIMITED or CLOSED

* **No safe power?** **Close**: lock doors, cone DT, keep a greeter.
* **30‑min food check:** Record temps (external probe if installed) **without opening** doors.
  **Proof:** Limited‑menu/closure photo + 30‑min temp entry.

### >60 MIN — PROTECT INVENTORY

* **Discard rules (simple):**

  * **Cold TCS >4°C (40°F) for >2h → DISCARD.**
  * **Hot TCS <60°C (140°F) for >2h → DISCARD.**
  * **Freezers:** keep shut; if thawed & >4°C for >2h → **DISCARD**.
* Log item, qty, reason, photo.

---

## ROLES (ODD)

* **AMOD (Owner):** Calls play, comms, decisions. **DoD:** Safety secured • Utility ticket • Ops notified • AAR done.
* **Food Safety Lead:** Seal units, temp/discard logs.
* **Cash/POS Lead:** Manual sales, refunds, cash reconcile.

> Short‑staffed? AMOD covers; **still complete each DoD.**

---

## SCRIPTS (USE AS‑IS)

* **Front Door:** “We’re experiencing a temporary power outage. For safety we’re pausing service. Utility ETA: ____. Thank you.”
* **Drive‑thru:** “Power outage — we’re closed/limited menu. Back as soon as it’s safe.”
* **Ops Chat:** “Store __ power loss at **:**. Utility ticket #____, ETA ____. Gen: Y/N. Status: Limited/Closed. Next update 30m.”

---

## CASH & POS (BARE MINIMUM)

* Use **Manual Sales Log**: time • items • price • tax • tender • initials.
* Keep receipts with the log.
* On restore, batch‑enter as **Outage Manual** tender and reconcile.

---

## AFTER‑ACTION (AAR) — DO WITHIN 24H

1. Duration (start/restore)  2) Any incidents  3) Inventory loss (photos)
2. Manual vs POS variance  5) **Add one fix** • **Remove one step** • **Clarify one DoD**
